Things do not always go as planned. Sometimes a judge makes a ruling that is not in your favor and other times the evidence does not support the ultimate findings. For many people facing these situations, the tendency is to give up hope. These moments cause fear, anger, hopelessness and confusion of what went wrong.

At Willoughby & Associates, we know that in these critical moments the battle is not over. We bring with us over 20 years of experience in fighting in front of the appellate courts. We have fought to change laws and made new law in the process. We are prepared to fight for your interests to the highest courts. No matter the issue you face, we are ready and able to effectively argue your interests before the California Court of Appeal and the Supreme Court.
