Civil Rights

In the United States, our rights must be protected. Civil rights are the class of rights that protect our individual freedom from infringement by the government, organizations, and private individuals. They ensure that we can peacefully go about our lives in the manner we see fit without fear of discrimination.

At Willoughby & Associates, we believe in fighting for your right to a safe life free of discrimination and oppression. We have fought for the right to a fair trial, due process, the right to seek civil participation, the right to vote, gun rights, the right to assemble, and most of all, the right to protect your home.

We fully believe that it is the duty of the legal profession to fight for these constitutional rights and the human rights that we are each inherently born with regardless of our country of origin. If you feel your rights have been violated, we are the firm that will stand at your side. We are your sword and shield.
